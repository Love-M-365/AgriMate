Learn how to sell your harvest efficiently and make the most of your produce.

Selling Options

1. eNAM (National Agriculture Market)

eNAM connects farmers with buyers nationwide. It's a great way to reach more people.

  • How It Works: List your harvest on eNAM and let traders bid on it.
  • Required Docs: ID proof (Aadhaar), bank details, produce certificates.
  • Benefits: National market access, better prices, and secure payments.

2. Private Mandis

Private mandis are local or regional markets where you can sell your harvest more quickly.

  • How It Works: Direct transactions with buyers or traders.
  • Required Docs: Basic ID and produce certificates.
  • Benefits: Faster transactions and personalized deals.

3. Local Mandis

Sell your produce at the nearest mandi based on its schedule.

  • How It Works: Visit the mandi on the scheduled date and sell directly.
  • Required Docs: Minimal (usually just farmer ID).

Important Notes

  • Verify Documents: Make sure your ID and any required certificates are ready.
  • Prepare Your Harvest: Organize your produce for easier sale.
  • Maintain Records: Keep communication records for digital sales.
