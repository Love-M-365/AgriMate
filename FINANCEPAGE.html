Agritech Startups

Agritech, or agricultural technology, startups are companies that leverage technology to improve efficiency, productivity, and sustainability in agriculture. These startups address various aspects of farming, from precision agriculture and supply chain improvements to innovative approaches in crop management, soil health, and more.

Contract Farming

Contract farming is an agricultural production system in which farmers and buyers (often agribusiness companies, food processors, or exporters) enter into an agreement or contract to produce a specific quantity and quality of crops or livestock. This arrangement outlines terms like pricing, delivery schedules, and quality standards before the farming even begins.
